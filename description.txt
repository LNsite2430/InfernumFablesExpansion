An expansion mod for Experimental Infernum Extension.

Experimental Infernum Extension Expansion adds boss intro presentations to many bosses that were previously missing them.

What it does:
- Adds intro cards to unsupported bosses
- Generates fitting titles for bosses without dedicated text

Requirements:
- InfernumFables
- CalamityFables
- InfernumMode
- CalamityMod

This is an add-on mod. It does not work by itself.
Adds generic boss name intros for bosses not covered by InfernumFables.
